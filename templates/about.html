About Us

Blending cutting-edge engineering with digital innovation to create lasting value for our communities.

Calmic Sdn Bhd, established in 2022 and headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, is a dynamic engineering consultancy and solutions provider dedicated to delivering innovative, high-quality services across multiple sectors.

With a team of licensed professional engineers and certified technicians, we specialize in water treatment (including desalination plants and marine desludging), security systems (CCTV and drone surveillance), telecommunications (fiber optic networks), and integrated infrastructure solutions.
