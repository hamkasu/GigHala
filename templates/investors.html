The Problem

The gig economy runs on broken trust.

Every transaction between a client and a freelancer carries unresolved risk — on both sides. No existing platform in Malaysia solves this end-to-end.

💸
Freelancers face payment risk

Unpaid invoices and scope creep are endemic. Workers have no legal recourse and no escrow protection on general platforms.

🎲
Clients face trust & quality risk

Unverified profiles, inconsistent delivery, and no accountability. Hiring a freelancer is still largely a leap of faith.

Global platforms ignore local context — Bahasa Malaysia, Ringgit, halal compliance, and Malaysia-specific regulations.
